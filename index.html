About Soul Safari

A slower rhythm in the Zululand bush.

Soul Safari is a considered three-day gathering held on ancient land within deep cultural roots. It brings together wellness, wilderness and quiet celebration in a setting designed for presence and connection.

Guided practices, safari moments, soulful music and thoughtful dining unfold gently across the weekend. The result is simple: room to breathe, reconnect and return home restored.
